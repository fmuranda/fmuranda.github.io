
## Features

- **Climate Modelling and Carbon Accounting:** Projects focused on climate workflows, GHG accounting, and emissions scenario analysis.  
- **Climate Data Science and AI:** Applying AI and data science to climate and ESG datasets for predictive modeling and decision support.  
- **ESG and Sustainability Projects:** Sustainability reporting, ESG assurance, and climate disclosure aligned with international frameworks.  
- **Data Analysis and Visualization:** Reusable workflows, scripts, and notebooks for cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing climate, ESG, and sustainability data.  
- **Academic Research:** Master’s research project on spatiotemporal analysis of climate variables under ENSO influence in Chimanimani, Zimbabwe (2015–2024).
